Title: Greenwood Valley High School – Academic and Administrative Records

Greenwood Valley High School is a private co-educational institution established in 2002. The school follows the State Board curriculum and emphasizes academic excellence, discipline, and extracurricular development.

Academic Structure

The school offers education from Grade 1 to Grade 12. Academic years are divided into two terms:

Term 1: June to October

Term 2: November to March

Each term includes periodic tests, assignments, and a final examination.

Departments

The school has the following academic departments:

Science Department
Includes Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Environmental Science. Practical laboratory sessions are conducted weekly.

Mathematics Department
Focuses on algebra, geometry, statistics, and calculus for higher grades.

Languages Department
Offers English, Hindi, and Sanskrit. Students must choose at least two languages.

Social Studies Department
Covers History, Geography, Civics, and Economics.

Computer Science Department
Teaches programming fundamentals, databases, networking, and cybersecurity basics from Grade 6 onwards.

Student Evaluation

Students are evaluated based on:

Written examinations (60%)

Assignments and projects (20%)

Class participation and attendance (10%)

Practical and lab work (10%)

A minimum of 35% is required in each subject to pass.

Staff Details

The school employs:

1 Principal

2 Vice Principals

45 Teaching Staff

8 Administrative Staff

10 Support and Maintenance Staff

Facilities

Science laboratories

Computer labs with 60 systems

Sports ground and indoor sports hall

Library with over 12,000 books

School buses covering major city routes

School Timings

Monday to Friday: 8:30 AM – 3:30 PM

Saturday: 8:30 AM – 12:30 PM
