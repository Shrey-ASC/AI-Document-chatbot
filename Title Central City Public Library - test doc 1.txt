Title: Central City Public Library – Internal Records Overview

The Central City Public Library is a government-funded public institution established in 1985 to promote reading, research, and community learning. The library operates six days a week and serves thousands of members annually, including students, researchers, professionals, and senior citizens.

Library Sections

The library is divided into multiple sections:

General Books Section
This section contains fiction and non-fiction books across genres such as mystery, fantasy, romance, biographies, self-help, philosophy, and travel. Books are arranged alphabetically by author name.

Reference Section
Reference materials include encyclopedias, dictionaries, atlases, government reports, and academic handbooks. These books are strictly for in-library use and cannot be issued.

Digital Resources Section
This section provides access to e-books, online journals, research databases, and archived newspapers. Members must log in using their membership ID to access these resources.

Children’s Section
Contains illustrated storybooks, educational books, comics, and learning kits for children aged 3 to 12 years. Weekly storytelling sessions are conducted here.

Periodicals Section
Houses newspapers, magazines, and monthly journals. Newspapers are archived for up to 10 years.

Membership Details

There are three types of library memberships:

Student Membership
Valid for 1 year. Allows borrowing up to 3 books for 14 days.

Regular Membership
Valid for 2 years. Allows borrowing up to 5 books for 21 days.

Premium Membership
Valid for 3 years. Allows borrowing up to 8 books for 30 days and provides access to exclusive digital resources.

Late return of books results in a fine of ₹5 per day per book. Damaged or lost books must be replaced or compensated based on current market value.

Library Staff

The library staff includes:

1 Chief Librarian

3 Assistant Librarians

6 Library Assistants

2 Digital Resource Managers

4 Maintenance and Support Staff

Operating Hours

Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM – 7:00 PM

Saturday: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM

Sunday: Closed

The library remains closed on national holidays and during annual inventory audits conducted every December.
